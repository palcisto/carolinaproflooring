About Us

Carolina Pro Flooring has its roots in a two-decade, family-shared passion for excellence and beauty in wood flooring.
Rafael Ferreira, at the heart of Carolina Pro Flooring, discovered his own vision for providing quality custom wood flooring designs while apprenticing under his father and uncles.

The collective wisdom and experience of his family, along with valuable opportunities to work with other established flooring companies, has given Rafael insight and knowledge into the best practices for the craft including material selection, treatment, design, and installation. These experiences, combined with a desire to provide highest quality wood flooring service, led to the creation of Carolina Pro Flooring, or CPF.

Our Vision

The flooring industry is full of innovative designers offering high-quality, elegant solutions to the market’s wood flooring needs. Unfortunately, the highest level of artistic attention to beautiful floors comes at a high price. For those whose budgets cannot reach high-end service, the only option is to compromise and to choose that which is affordable rather than that which they envision for their homes and offices.

CPF aims to bridge the gap between high-quality wood flooring services and affordability.
We wish to offer client-centered services that provide you with ease, peace of mind, and confidence in knowing that your floors are in the hands of professionals that do not compromise quality. We believe that when your priorities become our own, the result will be floors that you will love.

We do it right the first time. When doing your floors, don’t think twice — call Carolina Pro Flooring and have peace of mind!
